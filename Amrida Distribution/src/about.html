WHO WE ARE !

Armida Distribution, based in the heart of Quito, Ecuador, is a specialist distributor of high-end products with over three decades of experience. Our company has achieved remarkable growth over the past 15 years, thanks to our discerning end users, skilled resellers, motivated staff, and strong partnerships with renowned suppliers. Since our establishment in 2004, our office, warehouse, and training center have been based in Quito, allowing us to maintain close proximity to our valued customers and suppliers.

COUNTRY OVERVIEW

Ecuador is located in the western corner at the top of the South American continent. Ecuador is named after the Equator, the imaginary line around the Earth that splits the country in two. Most of the country is in the Southern Hemisphere.Ecuador had an estimated population of around 17.6 million people.Ecuador's economy is largely dependent on oil exports, which account for a significant portion of its revenue. Other important sectors include agriculture, manufacturing, and tourism.
